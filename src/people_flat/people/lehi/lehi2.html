Before 600 B.C. Lehi is a descendant of Manasseh who lives at Jerusalem and has acquired great possessions.

600 B.C. In response to the warning of prophets, in the first year of the reign of King Zedekiah of Judah, Lehi prays to God, is shown visions, and is called by God to preach to the people. They reject his message and seek to kill him. He flees Jerusalem with his family.

596-587 B.C. Lehi and his family are led through the wilderness and across the sea to a land of promise.

Circa 585 B.C. Lehi dies.
